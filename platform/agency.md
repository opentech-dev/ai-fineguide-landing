# Agency

The Agency section allows you to enroll in and manage your agency partnership program. This feature enables you to offer AI assistant services to your clients while maintaining your own branding and business identity.

## Overview

The Agency page provides tools for enrolling in the agency program, managing your agency profile, and overseeing client relationships. This is a premium feature that requires specific subscription permissions.

## Access Requirements

Agency features require specific subscription permissions. If you don't have access to the Agency section, you'll see an upgrade prompt explaining how to enable this feature.

## Features

### Agency Enrollment

Before you can use agency features, you need to complete the enrollment process:

1. **Initial Advertisement**: When first accessing the Agency section, you'll see an informational page explaining the benefits of the agency program.

2. **Enrollment Form**: After proceeding from the advertisement, you'll need to complete an enrollment form with details about your agency:
   - Agency name and contact information
   - Business details and industry focus
   - Client portfolio information
   - Service offerings and specializations
   - Branding assets (logo, color scheme)

3. **Approval Process**: After submitting your enrollment form, your application will be reviewed. During this time, you can check the status of your application in the Agency section.

4. **Approval Confirmation**: Once approved, you'll gain access to the full agency features and can begin managing clients under your agency account.

### Agency Dashboard

After approval, the Agency section transforms into a dashboard where you can:

- View key metrics about your agency performance
- Monitor client activity and engagement
- Track usage across all client accounts
- Access agency-specific resources and support

### Client Management

As an agency partner, you can manage multiple client accounts:

- Create new client accounts with customized settings
- Configure assistants specifically for each client
- Monitor client-specific analytics and performance
- Manage client billing and subscription details
- Set client-specific branding and customization

### Agency Branding

Customize the appearance of client-facing elements:

- Upload your agency logo and brand assets
- Set brand colors and styling preferences
- Create branded templates for client communications
- Customize client portals with your agency branding

### Agency Settings

Configure various aspects of your agency account:

- Update your agency profile information
- Manage team members and access permissions
- Configure notification preferences
- Set default settings for new client accounts

## Best Practices

- Complete your agency profile thoroughly to expedite the approval process
- Regularly review client performance metrics to identify opportunities for improvement
- Maintain consistent branding across all client touchpoints
- Use client-specific analytics to provide tailored recommendations
- Keep your agency information up to date as your business evolves

## Support

Agency partners receive priority support. If you encounter any issues or have questions about the agency program:

1. Check the Agency FAQ section for common questions
2. Contact your dedicated agency support representative
3. Use the agency support chat for immediate assistance
