# Platform Documentation

Welcome to the AI Backoffice Platform documentation. This section provides comprehensive information about all the features and components of the platform.

## About Fineguide

Fineguide is a comprehensive AI-powered customer engagement platform that enables businesses to create, deploy, and manage intelligent virtual assistants across multiple channels. The platform combines advanced conversational AI with robust backend management tools, allowing businesses to deliver personalized, efficient customer service at scale while maintaining human oversight when needed.

### Who is it for?

Fineguide serves:

- **Businesses of all sizes** seeking to enhance customer engagement and support
- **Customer service teams** looking to scale operations without proportionally increasing costs
- **Marketing departments** aiming to capture and qualify leads through conversational interfaces
- **Agencies and consultants** who manage client relationships and want to enhance customer service capabilities
- **Call centers** seeking to improve quality assurance and agent performance

### Problems Fineguide Solves

#### For Businesses
- **Customer Service Bottlenecks**: Automates responses to common inquiries, reducing wait times and freeing human agents for complex issues
- **Inconsistent Customer Experience**: Ensures standardized, on-brand responses across all customer touchpoints
- **After-Hours Support Gaps**: Provides 24/7 customer assistance without staffing constraints
- **Lead Capture Inefficiencies**: Engages visitors in real-time, qualifying leads before human follow-up

#### For Customer Service Teams
- **Agent Overwhelm**: Reduces repetitive inquiries handled by human agents
- **Knowledge Management**: Centralizes information for consistent, accurate responses
- **Quality Assurance**: Monitors call center performance with automated evaluation tools
- **Seamless Escalation**: Enables smooth handoff from AI to human agents when necessary

#### For Agencies
- **Client Management**: Offers multi-client management capabilities in a unified dashboard
- **Service Expansion**: Provides AI solutions as an additional service offering to clients
- **Performance Tracking**: Delivers analytics and insights on client engagement metrics

### Unique Selling Proposition

Fineguide stands apart through its:

1. **Comprehensive Integration**: Seamlessly connects with multiple channels including websites, WhatsApp, and call centers

2. **Human-in-the-Loop Design**: Unlike purely automated solutions, Fineguide enables human intervention when AI confidence is low or when customers request human assistance

3. **Advanced Analytics**: Provides detailed insights into customer interactions, agent performance, and conversation outcomes

4. **Customizable AI Assistants**: Offers extensive personalization options for virtual assistants, including appearance, tone, knowledge base, and behavior

5. **Advanced Voice Quality Assurance**: Industry-leading automated call center evaluation system that connects directly to your PBX, analyzes agent performance against predefined scripts and rules, and provides detailed compliance scoring with actionable insights

6. **Agency-Friendly Structure**: Comprehensive multi-client management dashboard makes it ideal for agencies serving multiple businesses

7. **No-Code Configuration**: Intuitive interface allows non-technical users to create and manage sophisticated AI assistants without development resources

## Main Features

### [Dashboard](/platform/dashboard)
Get a comprehensive overview of your platform's performance with real-time insights into assistant activity, conversation metrics, and usage statistics. Monitor key performance indicators and make data-driven decisions.

### [Assistants](/platform/assistants)
Create, configure, and manage AI assistants tailored to your specific needs. Customize their knowledge base, personality, and capabilities to provide the best possible experience for your users.

### [Leads](/platform/leads)
Capture and manage potential customer information gathered through interactions with your AI assistants. Track lead sources, export lead data, and integrate with your CRM systems.

### [Conversations](/platform/conversations)
Access and analyze all conversations between users and your AI assistants. Filter by date, assistant, or content to gain insights into user interactions and assistant performance.

### [Tickets](/platform/tickets)
Manage support tickets created from conversations that require human intervention. Assign tickets to team members, track their status, and ensure timely resolution.

### [Widgets](/platform/widgets)
Configure and deploy chat widgets on your website to provide instant AI assistance to your visitors. Customize appearance, behavior, and integration options.

### [VoiceQA](/platform/voiceqa)
Monitor and improve the quality of voice interactions with comprehensive quality assurance tools. Analyze conversations, identify patterns, and optimize voice assistant performance.

### [Analytics](/platform/analytics)
Gain deep insights into platform usage, assistant performance, and user engagement with detailed analytics and reporting tools. Visualize trends and identify opportunities for improvement.

### [Billing](/platform/billing)
Manage your subscription, view invoices, and monitor usage to ensure you're on the right plan for your needs. Update payment methods and access billing history.

### [Agency](/platform/agency)
Access specialized tools for agencies to manage multiple client accounts, customize branding, and streamline client onboarding and management.

### [Chats](/platform/chats)
Engage in real-time conversations with users through the platform's chat interface. Monitor active chats and intervene when necessary.

### [Settings](/platform/settings)
Configure your account preferences, manage integrations with external services, and customize notification settings to personalize your experience.
