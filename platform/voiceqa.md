# VoiceQA

The VoiceQA section provides tools for monitoring, analyzing, and improving voice interactions between your AI assistants and users. This feature helps ensure high-quality voice communications and provides insights into conversation performance.

## Overview

The VoiceQA page is organized into six main tabs:

1. **Statistics** - Overview of voice interaction metrics
2. **Conversations** - Detailed view of voice conversations
3. **Departments** - Manage department structures for voice interactions
4. **Staff** - Manage staff members who handle voice interactions
5. **Clients** - View and manage client information
6. **Settings** - Configure VoiceQA preferences and parameters

## Access Requirements

VoiceQA is a premium feature that requires specific subscription permissions. If you don't have access to VoiceQA, you'll see an upgrade prompt explaining how to enable this feature.

## Features

### Statistics

The Statistics tab provides an overview of your voice interaction performance:

- Call volume trends over time
- Average call duration
- Call quality scores
- Success rates and customer satisfaction metrics
- Performance comparisons across departments and staff

Use these metrics to identify trends, spot issues, and measure improvements in your voice interaction quality.

### Conversations

The Conversations tab allows you to:

- Browse through recorded voice interactions
- Listen to conversation recordings
- View automated transcripts of calls
- See quality assessments and ratings
- Filter conversations by date, staff member, department, or quality score

This tab is particularly useful for training purposes and quality control.

### Departments

The Departments tab helps you organize your voice interaction structure:

- Create and manage department hierarchies
- Assign staff members to departments
- Set department-specific quality standards
- View department-level performance metrics
- Configure routing rules for incoming voice interactions

### Staff

The Staff tab allows you to manage team members who handle voice interactions:

- Add and remove staff profiles
- Assign staff to departments
- View individual performance metrics
- Set training and quality improvement goals
- Monitor progress over time

### Clients

The Clients tab provides information about the users interacting with your voice assistants:

- View client profiles and interaction history
- See client-specific metrics and preferences
- Manage client categories and segments
- Configure personalization options for specific clients

### Settings

The Settings tab allows you to configure various aspects of the VoiceQA system:

- Quality assessment criteria
- Recording and storage preferences
- Notification settings
- Integration options with other systems
- Reporting frequency and formats

## Best Practices

- Regularly review the Statistics tab to identify trends and areas for improvement
- Use the Conversations tab to find examples of both excellent and problematic interactions
- Organize your departments logically to match your organizational structure
- Provide staff with regular feedback based on their performance metrics
- Configure settings to align with your privacy policies and regulatory requirements
- Use client data to personalize and improve future interactions
