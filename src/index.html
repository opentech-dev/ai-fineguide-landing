Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started with Fineguide AI

How quickly can I deploy an AI agent?

You can have a fully functional AI agent live on your website in under 5 minutes. Simply choose a template, upload your knowledge base (FAQs, docs, etc.), and embed the widget. No coding or technical setup required.

What makes Fineguide different from other chatbots?

Unlike basic chatbots, Fineguide combines AI chat with voice capabilities (VoiceQA), advanced lead qualification, and seamless CRM integration. Our agents understand context, qualify leads intelligently, and integrate with 40+ languages and popular tools like Salesforce, HubSpot, and Zapier.

Is my data secure and compliant?

Absolutely. We're GDPR compliant and maintain 99.9% uptime with enterprise-grade security. Your customer data is encrypted, never shared with third parties, and you maintain full control over all interactions and analytics.

What integrations are available?

Fineguide integrates with popular CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive), communication tools (Slack, Teams), automation platforms (Zapier, Make), and supports webhooks for custom integrations. We also offer white-label solutions for agencies.

What kind of support do you provide?

All plans include email support and comprehensive documentation. Professional plans get priority support, while Enterprise customers receive dedicated account management and custom onboarding. Our average response time is under 2 hours during business hours.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties or cancellation fees. Your AI agent will remain active until the end of your current billing period, and you can export all your data and conversation history.
