How Our Pricing Model Works

Fineguide uses a credit-based system to give you maximum flexibility when working with different AI models. Here's how it works:

Standardized Credits

Credits are used to standardize usage across various large language models (LLMs) from top vendors like OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, and more.

Credits Per Message

Each message you send consumes credits based on the model you choose. More powerful models may use more credits per message.

Monthly Allowance

Every plan includes a monthly credit allowance. These credits renew every month and can be used with any supported model.

Extra Credits

Need more? If you use up your credits, you automatically move into extra credits, billed at your plan's overage rate.

Better Value with Higher Plans

Higher plans come with lower extra credit costs, giving you better value as your usage grows.
