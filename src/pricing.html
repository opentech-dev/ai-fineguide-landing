Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our credit-based pricing system.

How do credits work?

Each AI text message costs 1 credit. Messages with file or image attachments cost 2 credits total (1 for the message + 1 for the attachment). VoiceQA analysis uses 12 credits per minute. Document processing, analytics, and integrations are included at no additional credit cost.

Do credits expire?

Monthly plan credits reset each billing cycle. Top-up credit packages never expire, giving you complete flexibility for irregular usage patterns.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your monthly plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle. You can also purchase top-up credits anytime for immediate use.

What happens if I run out of credits?

You can purchase additional credits instantly through top-up packages. Your AI agents will continue working, and you'll be notified when credits are running low.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All new accounts start with free credits to test the platform. You can explore all features including AI conversations, VoiceQA, and integrations before choosing a paid plan.
