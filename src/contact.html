Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about getting started with Fineguide

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM EST, Monday-Friday). For urgent matters, please mention it in your message.

Can I schedule a demo instead?

Absolutely! You can schedule a personalized demo to see Fineguide in action. Our product experts will walk you through the platform based on your specific needs.

What information should I include in my message?

Please include your company name, current customer support challenges, and what you're hoping to achieve with AI automation. The more details you provide, the better we can tailor our response to your needs.
